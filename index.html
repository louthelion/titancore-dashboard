Approval Flow

1. Company departments work the contract / package.
2. Company Manager + Assistant Manager + Administrator review and sign internally.
3. Package goes to TitanCore departments for full review.
4. If something is missing, TitanCore returns correction notes to the company.
5. Company departments fix the issue and resend it through company leadership.
6. TitanCore departments review again.
7. TitanCore Manager + Assistant Manager + Administrator sign internally.
8. TitanCore sends the approved package back to the company.
9. CEO performs the final sign.
10. Company sends the final signed package to the client.
